Tango Elo is an e-sports inspired Japanese word/kanji quiz that uses an Elo system to rank and guess the size of your vocabulary.
The quiz contains 120,247 words which are ranked based on their frequency.
Multiple frequency lists were used and each words ranking has been generated by averaging it's ranking from each list.
My hope is that by using multiple lists from different sources the vocabulary will be less skewed towards one specific domain.
Some sources were weighted less heavily in the average rank due to highly specific and rare vocabulary having exceedingly high frequency rates. (Wikimedia)

The Frequency lists were sourced from the following;
- Aozora Bunko
- JPDB
- Youtube
- Anime and J-Drama
- Wikimedia
- Jmdict

It's important to note that for the most part, words are not sorted by perceived difficulty, but instead by frequency of use.
So you may find "easy" words that have a high rarity, and "difficult" words with low rarity.

The estimations of vocab and kanji count are not to be taken too seriously.
I've tried my best to make them represenative and give a decent ballpark estimate, but in the end this app is intended as entertainment.
