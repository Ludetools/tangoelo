# Data Attribution

This app uses derived data from the JMdict/EDICT Japanese dictionary project.

JMdict is maintained by the Electronic Dictionary Research and Development Group
(EDRDG). The dictionary data is distributed under a Creative Commons
Attribution-ShareAlike license.

Project page: https://www.edrdg.org/wiki/index.php/JMdict-EDICT_Dictionary_Project

The app uses a compact derived subset in `jmdict-frequency-data.js` to provide
readings, English glosses, and filtering metadata for quiz cards.
